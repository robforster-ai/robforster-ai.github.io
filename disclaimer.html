About This Website

This website — including its design, structure, written content, blog articles, use case descriptions, KPI frameworks, news summaries, tools, and all other materials — has been produced with the assistance of artificial intelligence, specifically Anthropic’s Claude.

The views, insights, frameworks and recommendations expressed across this site reflect the professional perspective of Rob Forster, Enterprise AI Transformation Lead. The AI has been used as a tool to help articulate, structure and present those perspectives — not to replace them.

What This Means in Practice

Every piece of content on this site was conceived and directed by a human. AI was used to draft, refine and format that content, in the same way a professional might use a writing tool, a design application or a research assistant.

The blog articles represent genuine practitioner opinion on enterprise AI delivery. The use case library, KPI frameworks and ROI calculator are based on real-world programme experience. The curated news section links to original third-party sources which are not AI-generated.

Accuracy and Currency

While every effort has been made to ensure the accuracy of information on this site, content may not reflect the most recent developments in a fast-moving field. Nothing on this site constitutes professional advice. Readers should apply their own judgement and seek appropriate expertise before making decisions based on any content found here.

The ROI calculator and AI Use Case Recommender produce illustrative estimates only. Figures are indicative and should not be relied upon as the basis for business decisions without further analysis.
